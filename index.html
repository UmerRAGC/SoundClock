Auditory Time Encoding. Uses the official high-fidelity sounds from the SoundClock app. Designed for nighttime awareness, focus, and digital detox. Settings are saved locally in your browser.
How it works

This clock speaks time using short sound “phrases” that repeat every 15s: Hour (Cat A-D) → Ten‑minutes (Cat E-F) → One‑minute digit (Cat A-D) → Ping (Cat G).

  • Cuts encode position: no cut = 1st of block, 1 cut = 2nd, 2 cuts = 3rd.
  • Hour block cycles every 3 hours across 4 distinct environment sounds.
  • Ten‑minutes uses singing bowls/rain markers with 0/1/2 cuts.
  • One‑minute digit uses environment markers (0-9).
  • Ping signals the end of the phrase.
